About Synca

Syncing you up with the perfect place to call home

Synca is a modern PG and hostel booking platform designed to make accommodation search simple, transparent, and hassle-free for students and young professionals.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the PG and hostel booking experience by providing a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform that connects students and young professionals with quality accommodations.

Speed & Efficiency

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We're here to support you throughout your accommodation journey.

Our Story

Synca was born from a simple idea: finding quality accommodation shouldn't be a stressful experience. As students ourselves, we understood the challenges of searching for PGs and hostels.

We noticed the lack of transparency, outdated processes, and difficulty in finding accurate information about available beds. This inspired us to create a platform that would solve these problems.

Today, Synca brings together property owners and students on a single platform, making the entire process seamless, transparent, and trustworthy.
